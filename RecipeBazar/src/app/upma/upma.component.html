Upma

South indian breakfast



Upma recipe



Ingredients

  • Semolina(suji) - 2 cups
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Cumin - 1tbsp
  • Green Chilli - 2 chopped
  • Red chilli - 2
  • Peanuts - 2 tbsp
  • Bengal gram(chana dal) - 2 tbsp
  • Cashew (optional) - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - According to taste
  • Water - 2 cups

Preparation

  • Saute semolina until aroma comes on low flame
  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Add cumin, green chilli and red chilli and saute for a minute
  • Add peanuts, cashew and chana dal and fry until golden brown
  • Add water and stir
  • Add salt as needed
  • Bring the water to boil
  • Add sauteed semolina to the mixure and stir continuously to avoid any lumps
  • Close the lid and cook for 10 min
